Welcome to the Online Video Comparison Player

This interactive tool allows you to compare video files directly in your web browser without the need for uploads, ensuring quick load times and enhanced privacy.

Key Features

  • Overlay Mode: Overlay one video over another with adjustable transparency to see detailed differences.
  • Comparison Mode: View videos side-by-side for straightforward comparison.
  • Local File Handling: All video processing is done locally for improved privacy and speed.
  • Interactive Controls:
    • Play, pause, and mute for precise video control.
    • Draggable progress bar for easy navigation.
    • Adjust transparency and sync videos with time offsets.
  • Responsive and Intuitive UI: Built with Bootstrap for a seamless experience on any device.

Benefits for Users

  • Privacy and Security: Your videos stay on your device, ensuring they remain private and secure.
  • Versatility: Ideal for video editors, content creators, educators, and more.
  • Accessibility: Easy to use, no technical skills required.
